## WHY DIRECTORY LISTINGS MATTER

Google uses **citations** (mentions of your business Name, Address, Phone — NAP) as a ranking signal for local search. Consistent NAP across the web boosts your trust and visibility in the Local Pack.

Your **NAP Format** (use EXACTLY this everywhere):
- **Name**: Maher Electrical
- **Address**: Mountmellick, Co. Laois, Ireland
- **Phone**: +353 85 200 8988 (or 085 200 8988)
- **Website**: https://maherelectrical.ie

## TIER 1: ESSENTIAL (Do These First)

### 1. Google Business Profile
- URL: https://business.google.com
- Status: See GOOGLE_BUSINESS_PROFILE_SETUP.md

### 2. Bing Places for Business
- URL: https://www.bingplaces.com
- Uses same info as Google. Often overlooked but drives Bing + Bing Maps traffic.

### 3. Facebook Business Page
- URL: https://business.facebook.com
- Create a Page (not a personal profile)
- Add address, phone, hours, WhatsApp button
- Post photos of work regularly

### 4. Apple Business Connect
- URL: https://businessconnect.apple.com
- Appears in Apple Maps — significant as many iPhone users search via Siri/Maps.
