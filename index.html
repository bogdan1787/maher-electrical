Why Choose Maher Electrical?

Maher Electrical is a registered electrical contractor based in Mountmellick, Co. Laois, run by Conn Maher. We bring years of hands-on experience to every project, from a simple socket replacement to full commercial fit-outs and SEAI-registered solar installations.

We are fully certified by ECSSA and SEAI, meaning our work meets the highest Irish safety and quality standards. Whether you need an emergency call-out at 9pm or a solar PV survey for your farm, we are available.

Our service area covers all of Co. Laois, extends into neighbouring Co. Offaly and Co. Kildare, and reaches into Carlow and Kilkenny for larger commercial and renewable energy projects.
